Interfaces for random number generation services

The package fr.upmc.components.examples.smoothing.rng/code> defines two kinds of random number generation components. The component UniformRNGenerator generates random numbers from a uniform distribution between lower and an upper bounds lb and up, aka U(lb, ub), while the component NormalRNGenerator generates random numbers from a normal distribution of mean 0 and standard deviation 1, aka N(0,1), using two U(0,1) random numbers.

These components make use of interfaces in the package fr.upmc.components.examples.smoothing.rng.interfaces/code> to offer their service and for their implementation of service methods.
